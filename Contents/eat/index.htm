Starbucks

Open daily from 4:30 a.m. to 9 p.m.
(907) 457-1801



Travelers can now enjoy the premier roaster and retailer of specialty coffee in the world at FAI. They offer freshly brewed coffee and espresso drinks, a wide selection of premium teas, fine pastries, sandwiches, and other delectable treats to please the taste buds. This location is the northernmost Starbucks in an airport in the world to date.

The Local @ FAI

Open daily from 6 a.m. to 1 a.m.
(907) 457-1804



Travelers can choose from a variety of appetizers such as Bavarian Pretzels with cheese fondue and Dirty Chips--kettle chips topped with brisket trimmings, garlic aioli, and scallions. Entree selections include flatbread pizzas and salads, as well as a variety of sandwiches such as the beef brisket and turkey sliders, and burgers such as the Country Burger topped with pulled pork, cheddar, and slaw. For early morning travelers, guests will find a selection of breakfast sandwiches, omelets, and skillets as well as grab-and-go baked goods.
